# Field

## Overview
4x8 foot board with two zones: the racecourse and the pit area.

Base material: **23/32 in. x 4 ft. x 8 ft. RTD Sheathing Syp**

---

## Zones

### Racecourse
- Oval track with two lanes
- Lane divider: 1 in. wide white center line (painted, not tape)
- Two long straightaways where obstacles are placed
- Total field width: 48 in.
- Total field height: 96 in.

### Pit Area
- Located at the bottom of the field
- 28 in. tall
- Three parking spots, each 12x12 in.
- Spots numbered 1, 2, 3
- Spots separated by 3 in. gaps with 2 in. margins on outer edges

---

## Key Markings

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Center line | 1 in. W x 36 in. L white, painted |
| Wall barriers | 1 in. W x 4 in. H, painted gray |
| Start/finish line | Checkerboard adhesive tape |
| Start flag mount | Wall separating pit from racecourse |
| Apriltag mount | Wall near start area |

---

## Colors

| Area | Color | Paint Code |
|---|---|---|
| Track surface | Black (Onyx) | PPG1011-7 |
| Walls and barriers | Gray (Winter Chill) | PPG1036-2 |
| Center line | White (Delicate White) | PPG1001-1 |

> Center line is painted white, covered with masking tape, then painted black. Remove tape to reveal white line.

---

## Tolerances
Field and game elements may vary by **± 1 inch (25.4 mm)** per match. Design your robot to handle this.

---

## Obstacles
- 2 obstacle cars placed randomly on the course
- One in the inner lane, one in the outer lane
- Each on a long straightaway
- Do not interact with them after placement
